Privacy Policy for Stackopolis

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This privacy policy explains how the mobile game Stackopolis ("the app", "we", "us") handles information. The app is developed by Jan de Bie. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect or store any personal information that identifies you on our own servers. The app has no servers of its own.

Your game progress (such as your high score, earned diamonds, unlocked eras and achievements) is saved only on your device using local storage. It never leaves your device, and we cannot see it. Uninstalling the app deletes this data.

2. Advertising

The app may show advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain data, such as a device advertising identifier, general (non-precise) location, IP address and information about your device and ad interactions. This is used to show and measure ads (personalised or non-personalised, subject to your choices and local law).

This data is collected by Google, not by us. Please review Google's policy to understand how it is used: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

3. Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will address it.

4. Data security & retention

Because game data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device. We do not retain any of your data ourselves. Advertising data is handled by Google under its own retention and security practices.

5. Your choices

  • Reset or delete your advertising ID via your device's privacy settings.
  • Opt out of personalised ads in your device's Google ad settings.
  • Delete all locally stored game data by uninstalling the app.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
